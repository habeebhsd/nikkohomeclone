    Why Are Floor Plans Important for Real Estate?

    Good floor plans increase customer engagement with the real estate listing and can even increase the resale value of the property. The 2021 Home Buyers and Sellers Report by National Association of Realtors (NAR) shows that 75% of home buyers search for homes online. Of these, more than half rate floor plans as being a “very useful” feature.

    According to research by Zillow, 81% of home buyers say they are more likely to view a home if the listing includes a floor plan they like. So get up on that competition and start creating floor plans for your listings.
